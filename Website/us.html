HÖHERE-TECHNISCHE-LEHRANSTALT-LEONDING

In general

The Higher Technical College Leonding is a highly appreciated school in which you can attend different computer related branches, it got founded 1984 and worked its way up to a really well managed and valueable school. Students who attended this school will have a really lucrative business-life, and also will get some things done.

Computer Sience

In this branch you will get in touch with different programming languages and the student is going to learn how to develop and code algorithms and different programs.

IT- Media Technology

Here you will encounter and improve your skills in Software Engineering, developing Web-technologies and modern mobile Applications with photos the student took himself in the lessons.

Electronics

You always wanted to know how to work with your hands to build robots, and powerful devices which can used for many different things and can also build in, for example a car.

Medical

If the basic understanding of Electronics is achieved, the students will lern to program and develop devices which should get used in the medical-branch like hospitals
